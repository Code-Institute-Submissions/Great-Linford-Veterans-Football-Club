Great Linford FC Lucky 7's

Date 12 September
Pot £595

At least 5 matches must take place for the competition to be valid. In the event of 3 or more postponements all entry fees will be refunded and the pot will not increase. In the event being won with less than 7 games being played then only 50% of the pot value will be awarded to the winner. In the event of more than one winner the pot will be equally distributed between winners You must correctly predict all 7 matches, home/away or draw to win. For any Cup matches the score at 90 minutes will count. For any abandoned matches the score at the time of the abandonment will count. The pot will increase by £5 every week if less than 35 players have entered until the pot is won. The pot will increase by £10 every week if 35 players or more have entered until the pot is won. All predictions must be submitted by 2.55pm on a Saturday. Entries after this time will not be valid. Predictions can be emailed to wellsman74@googlemail.com. Entry fee is £1 and you may enter as many times as you like, payment can be made in cash on day or via bank transfer

All proceeds after payment of prizes goes to Great Linford Football Club.
